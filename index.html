01 // About

Who I Am

I am a PhD Scholar in Engineering Science at the Academy of Scientific and Innovative Research (AcSIR), working as a Project Associate at CSIR-Advanced Materials and Processes Research Institute, Bhopal.

My research journey is driven by a passion for building intelligent sensing systems that address real-world challenges in water quality, environmental monitoring, and public health. I combine optical physics, nanomaterials, IoT, and AI to design next-generation sensors.

My career objective is to pursue a teaching faculty position where I can apply my academic background and research expertise to foster quality education, mentor students, and contribute to institutional academic excellence.

I hold an M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from MNNIT Prayagraj and a B.E. from Mumbai University. Prior to research, I worked as a Field Support Engineer with Indus Towers Limited in Mumbai.

04 // Research

Projects & Research Work

01
Smart Optical Sensing Device for Multi-Parameter Detection in Aquatic Sources with AI & Additive Manufacturing
Real-time centralized monitoring system for Cadmium and Arsenic using AI and CAD-CAE-CAM computer-aided technologies. Combines smart sensing with IoT for accurate water quality surveillance.
02
Optical Sensor for Detection of Arsenic in Water Resources
Developed a fluorescence-based smart optical sensor for arsenic detection — a globally significant water contamination challenge. Proposed novel receptor materials and sensing phenomena.
03
Manufacturing of Automatic Guided Hovercraft
Designed and manufactured an autonomously guided hovercraft with skirt, blower, propeller, and Arduino (UNO). Led coding and automation as team head; integrated sensors and actuators for full automation.
04
Deep Learning for Real-Time Melt Pool Classification in Additive Manufacturing
Developing a Deep Learning Model based on image classification to study meltpool of Laser Powder Bed Fusion(LPBF) to find out defects in additive manufacturing in real time
